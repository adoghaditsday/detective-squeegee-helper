 Important Notes

- This app can terminate processes — use responsibly
- Some actions may require Administrator privileges
- "Not Responding Duration" is tracked during runtime only
- Windows SmartScreen may warn because the app is unsigned

---

 Safety Design

Detective SqueeGee:
- Skips critical/system processes automatically
- Prompts before terminating processes
- Logs all termination actions
- Uses standard Windows APIs (no injection or unsafe hooks)

---

pip install -r requirements.txt
